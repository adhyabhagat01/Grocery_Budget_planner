# Grocery Budget Planner for Students

A specialized mobile-first application designed to help college students manage their grocery expenses, track consumption, and compare prices across popular quick-commerce platforms.

## 📌 Problem Statement
College students often face difficulties managing grocery expenses due to:
* **Limited Budgets:** Relying on irregular income or fixed monthly allowances.
* **Lack of Tracking:** Realizing overspending only at the end of the month.
* **Inefficiency:** Unintentional duplicate purchases and food wastage.
* **Price Disparity:** Struggling to identify the cheapest platform among Blinkit, Instamart, and Zepto.

## ✨ Key Features
* **Real-time Expense Tracking:** Record daily spending to maintain financial awareness.
* **Price Comparison:** Compare item prices across different quick-commerce platforms to maximize savings.
* **Grocery Usage Monitoring:** Track stock levels to avoid duplicate purchases.
* [cite_start**Visual Analytics:** Spending summaries through interactive pie charts and bar charts.
* **Budget Alerts:** Notifications when spending reaches a defined threshold (e.g., 80% of monthly budget).

## 🛠️ Technology Stack
* **Design:** Figma (Interactive Prototyping) 
* **Front-end:** React.js 
* **Routing:** React Router DOM 
* **Icons:** Lucide-React 
* **Styling:** CSS3 (including custom variables for a student-centric theme)

## 👤 User Personas
The app is designed around two primary student types:
1. **The Budget-Conscious (e.g., Riya Sharma):** Needs to stay strictly within a monthly allowance and avoid any wastage.
2. **The Busy Student (e.g., Arjun Patel):** Balancing work and study, seeking quick automated tracking and the best deals.

## 🧪 Results & Testing
Usability testing conducted with students revealed a high demand for a unified app (88% of surveyed students).
Feedback sessions led to the addition of budget alerts and a simplified navigation layout to reduce cognitive load.
